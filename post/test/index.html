二次多项式配平方式过程：

$$\begin{aligned} y&=ax^2+bx+c\quad(a\neq0)\\
&=a\left(x^2+\frac{b}{a}x+\frac{c}{a}\right)\\
&=a\left(\left( x+\frac{b}{2a}\right)^2-\frac{b^2}{4a^2}+\frac{c}{a}\right)\\
&=a\left(\left( x+\frac{b}{2a}\right)^2-\frac{b^2}{4a^2}+\frac{4ac}{4a^2}\right)\\
&=a\left(\left( x+\frac{b}{2a}\right)^2-\frac{b^2-4ac}{4a^2}\right)\quad(1)\\
&=a\left(\left( x+\frac{b}{2a}\right)^2+\frac{4ac-b^2}{4a^2}\right)\quad(2) \end{aligned}$$

由上述(2)式知二次函数顶点坐标为：

$$\left(-\frac{b}{2a},\frac{4ac-b^2}{4a^2}\right)$$

令函数值为0，求方程解：

$$\begin{aligned} y&=0\\
ax^2+bx+c&=0\\
a\left( x^2+\frac{b}{a}x+\frac{c}{a}\right)&=0\\
x^2+\frac{b}{a}x+\frac{c}{a}&=0\\
\left( x+\frac{b}{2a}\right)^2-\frac{b^2}{4a^2}+\frac{c}{a}&=0\\
\left( x+\frac{b}{2a}\right)^2&=\frac{b^2}{4a^2}-\frac{c}{a}\\
\left( x+\frac{b}{2a}\right)^2&=\frac{b^2-4ac}{4a^2}\\
x+\frac{b}{2a}&=\pm\sqrt{\frac{b^2-4ac}{4a^2}}\\
x&=-\frac{b}{2a}\pm\sqrt{\frac{b^2-4ac}{4a^2}}\\
x&=\frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\quad(b^2-4ac\ge0) \end{aligned}$$
